Units

UnitfulGaussian.Fr — Constant
UnitfulGaussian.Fr

The franklin, the CGS-ESU and Gaussian unit of charge.

Dimension: 𝐌^1/2 𝐋^3/2 𝐓^-1.

See also: Unitful.C

source
UnitfulGaussian.statA — Constant
UnitfulGaussian.statA

The statampere, the CGS-ESU and Gaussian unit of current, equal to 1 franklin per second.

Dimension: 𝐌^1/2 𝐋^3/2 𝐓^-2.

See also: Unitful.A

source
UnitfulGaussian.statV — Constant
UnitfulGaussian.statV

The statvolt, the CGS-ESU and Gaussian unit of electric potential.

Dimensions: 𝐌^1/2 𝐋^1/2 𝐓^-1.

See also: Unitful.V

source
UnitfulGaussian.G — Constant
UnitfulGaussian.G

The gauss, the CGS-EMU and Gaussian unit of magnetic B field.

Dimensions: 𝐌^1/2 𝐋^3/2 𝐓^-1.

See also: Unitful.T

Warning

Unitful.jl also defines a gauss as a 10⁻⁴ T. This is not dimensionally equivalent to the unit described here.

source
UnitfulGaussian.Mx — Constant
UnitfulGaussian.Mx

The maxwell, the CGS-EMU and Gaussian unit of magnetic flux (flux of the magnetic B-field).

Dimensions: 𝐌^1/2 𝐋^3/2 𝐓^-1.

See also: Unitful.Wb

Warning

Unitful.jl also defines a maxwell as 10⁻⁸ Wb. This is not dimensionally equivalent to the unit described here.

source
UnitfulGaussian.Oe — Constant
UnitfulGaussian.Oe

The oersted, the CGS-EMU and Gaussian unit of the magnetic H field. It is dimensionally equivalent to the gauss (G). The corresponding SI unit for the H field is A/m.

Dimensions: 𝐌^1/2 𝐋^3/2 𝐓^-1.

See also: Unitful.A

Warning

Unitful.jl also defines a oersted as 1000/4π A/m. This is not dimensionally equivalent to the unit described here.

source
UnitfulGaussian.statF — Constant
UnitfulGaussian.statF

The statfarad, the CGS-ESU and Gaussian unit of capacitance. It is equal to 1cm, and the cm should be used in the Gaussian system of units for capacitance.

Dimensions: 𝐋

See also: Unitful.F

source
UnitfulGaussian.statH — Constant
UnitfulGaussian.statH

The stathenry, the CGS-ESU and Gaussian unit of inductance. It is equal to 1s^2/cm.

Dimensions: 𝐋^-1 𝐓^2

See also: Unitful.H
